General Pricing

As a tutor, I charge $30 per hour for individual tutoring sessions. This rate includes personalized lesson planning, one-on-one instruction tailored to the student's needs, and access to additional learning resources as needed. I believe in providing value through quality education, and my pricing reflects the level of commitment and effort I put into each session.

Pacakges

I also offer package deals for students interested in booking multiple sessions in advance. For example, a package of 10 hours comes at a discounted rate of $250, providing a $50 savings compared to individual hourly rates. This option is popular among students who plan to have ongoing tutoring support throughout a semester or academic year.

Cancellation

Additionally, I have a flexible cancellation policy that allows students to reschedule or cancel sessions with at least 24 hours' notice without incurring any charges. I want to ensure that my tutoring services are both accessible and accommodating to students' needs.
