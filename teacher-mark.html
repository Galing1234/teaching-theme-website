Hi, I'm Mark Evans, 30 y/o, and father to 2 beautiful kids. I am teaching English in Toronto, CA. I believe in tutoring children in a fun way, and make them value themselves.
I'm passionate about education and helping students reach their full potential. With years of experience in tutoring, I have honed my skills and developed a deep understanding of various subjects. My mission is to empower students with knowledge and confidence, ensuring their academic success...
I tailor my lessons to each child's individual strengths and areas that need improvement. I take the time to get to know their interests and learning preferences, allowing me to customize the curriculum to suit their needs. This personalized approach ensures that every child feels supported and empowered to reach their full potential...
Tutoring has always held a special place in my heart because it played a crucial role in my own academic journey. Growing up, I faced my fair share of challenges in school. I struggled to grasp certain subjects and concepts, and it often left me feeling frustrated and disheartened...
As a tutor, I charge $30 per hour for individual tutoring sessions. This rate includes personalized lesson planning, one-on-one instruction tailored to the student's needs, and access to additional learning resources as needed. I believe in providing value through quality education, and my pricing reflects the level of commitment and effort I put into each session...
